Privacy Policy

Boise Custom Woodwork, LLC respects the privacy of all our customers. Any information you provide to us through the normal course of business will be used solely to better fulfill your order.

If you opt in for our periodic newsletter, we will contact you only with relevant news updates. We will happily maintain compliance with the CAN-SPAM act and honor any requests to opt-out (which is also our default option). We don't want to send you unwanted email any more than you want to receive it.

We will only share your completed project in our gallery if given explicit permission to do so and will not include any identifying marks or information when doing so.
